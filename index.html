About

Our History

Kia Ora and welcome to Poutama Solutions. We are a Māori-led consultancy committed to helping iwi, hapū and communities turn vision into real, practical development. From civil infrastructure to water supply, geothermal energy and complex project delivery, our team brings together deep expertise to support sustainable growth across Aotearoa.

Our focus is simple — strong foundations, strong communities, long-term impact. If you’re an iwi, marae, trust or organisation looking to move a project forward, let’s kōrero. The climb starts now.

Why Poutama?

We help unlock funding opportunities and deliver resilient solutions across key infrastructure areas.
